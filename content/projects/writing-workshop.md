## About

The Words4 Writing Lab is an exciting opportunity for anyone who loves writing or is interested in improving their writing. Participants in the program will learn how to craft their own stories with the support of Punahou Academy volunteers. The Writing Lab is for middle school students who want to develop writing skills or seek an opportunity to create original stories to be shared with the community.
