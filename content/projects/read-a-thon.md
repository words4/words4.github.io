## About

The Read-a-thon is a project encouraging children to read in a fun and interactive way. It is a competition; whoever reads the most wins, and the top three receive special prizes! Many children in Hawaii may not have access to quality education or books. When these kids grow up, they will still be unable to read or functionally illiterate. In response, Words4 has developed the Read-a-thon project to encourage children to read more in an attempt to circumvent this problem. By making reading enjoyable, we help children to read every day, improving their literacy, which benefits themselves and their peers.
