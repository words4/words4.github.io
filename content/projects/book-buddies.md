## About

Words4 is a reading organization, and our mission is to help more kids in Hawaii enjoy reading.

As a part of this mission, we created the Book Buddies Program. We send tutors to help young students do fun activities, like reading books and making bookmarks. This program helps them enjoy reading more or even love it, and have tons of fun over the summer!
